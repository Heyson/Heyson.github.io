EVOLUTION Tuesdays @ RumoR is the longest running weekly night in the city of Boston... Dating back to 1997, the name EVOLUTION has become its signature due to the various locations the party has taken place. The Tuesday night party evolved from Bills Bar, Aria, Trio, Whiskey Park, Embassy, and for the last 4 years has found its favorite home at RumoR. Since being at RumoR, Tuesday nights have been recongnized by The Improper Bostonian as Boston’s Best for the years of 06’, 07’ and 08’. Customers are always amazed at how many people attend this extraordinary party. The overall atmosphere and vibe that is created weekly is second to none.
